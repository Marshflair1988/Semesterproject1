Explore

Whether you are a young scientist, a teacher, or a researcher, there is something here for you.

For Kids

Are you a young person looking to learn more about science? Come down to our museum, there is plenty to see and do.

You can learn about Newtonian physics on our bumper swing, meet the woolly mammoth, and try out many interactive displays. Our exhibits are designed to be hands on and fun.

Young Stars Club

Every Saturday 10:00–13:00. Join other young explorers for experiments, activities, and discovery.

Holiday Clubs

During school holidays we run special themed learning programmes. Check our Events page for dates and themes.

For Teachers

We offer guided school tours that bring the curriculum to life. Your class can use our learning laboratory and attend educational presentations tailored to their age group.

We would love to work with you to create a visit that fits your learning goals. Please contact us to arrange a tour or discuss options.

For Researchers

Are you looking to get involved with our research or use our facilities? We work with universities and institutions on a range of projects.

We offer collaboration opportunities, access to laboratory space, and research resources. If you are interested in partnerships or using our collections and expertise, please get in touch.
