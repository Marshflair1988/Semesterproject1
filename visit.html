Admission

Entrance is free for all visitors.

Guided tours are available for groups and schools at 70 NOK per person. Please contact us to book a tour.

Accessibility

The museum is wheelchair accessible with ramps and lifts throughout. We also offer:

  • Wheelchair ramps at all entrances
  • Audio guides for selected exhibitions
  • Braille display signs

If you have specific access requirements, please get in touch and we will do our best to help.

Food and Drink

There is a museum café where you can enjoy light meals, snacks, and drinks. A great place to take a break between exhibitions.

Shop

Our museum shop offers a range of gifts, souvenirs, and science activity packs to continue the fun at home. Perfect for young scientists and curious minds.
