Exhibitions

Walk through our main exhibition spaces and discover the stories of science.

Cosmology

The Sky Above Us

Explore the wonders of our cosmos. Our exhibition takes you from the origins of the universe to the stars and galaxies we see today. Discover the forces that shape space and time.

Evolution

From Darwin to Dinosaurs

Discover the story of life on Earth. Follow Darwin’s theory of evolution and meet extinct animals including woolly mammoths and dinosaurs. See how species have changed over millions of years.

Biology and Medicine

Major Breakthroughs

From the discovery of penicillin to heart transplants, explore the major breakthroughs that have shaped modern medicine. See how biology and technology work together to save lives.

Robotics and AI

The Future Is Here

The future of technology is on display. Meet robots, explore artificial intelligence, and see how machines are changing the way we live and work.

Ecology

Our Planet and Sustainability

Learn about environmental science and sustainability. Discover how we can protect our planet and what role each of us can play in a greener future.
