Get Involved

Support the museum, volunteer your time, or start your career with us.

Support

There are many ways you can support the museum. Donations help us maintain our exhibits, develop new programmes, and keep entrance free for everyone.

You can also support us by donating items to our collection. If you have something you think would be of interest—from scientific instruments to historical artefacts—please contact us.

Volunteer

A great way to get involved is to volunteer. You can help maintain our exhibits, assist at staff lectures, or join our monthly lunch meetings where staff share their latest research.

If you would like to volunteer, we would love to hear from you. Get in touch and tell us how you would like to contribute.

Internships

Are you interested in working in a museum? We offer internship opportunities where you can learn from our academics and scientists. You will get hands-on experience in a real museum environment.

To find out more about current internship positions, please contact us.
