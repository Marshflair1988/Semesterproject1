Events

Special talks, family nights, and visiting exhibitions. See what is on this season.

Visiting Professor of Aeronautics

It is our pleasure to announce that Prof Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology will be delivering a series of lectures on aeronautics. From jet propulsion to the future of flight—do not miss this opportunity to hear from a leading expert.

Night in the Museum

Get your family together for an unforgettable night at the museum. Sleep beside the dinosaur exhibits and explore the halls after dark. A unique experience for children and adults alike. Check our Visit and Contact pages for dates and booking.

Energetica Exhibition on Loan

On loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam, the Energetica exhibition is coming to our museum. It features a series of installations that allow visitors to experience the forces of nature—from solar and wind power to the power of the human body. A hands-on exhibition for all ages.
